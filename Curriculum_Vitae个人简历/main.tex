  {Jul. 2023 - Jun. 2024}

\resumeItemListStart
\item \textbf{Developed a covalent model} for the THZ1-CAK complex (PDBID: 6XD3) incorporating non-standard amino acids, using CHARMM-GUI with the CHARMM force field for proteins and CGenFF for the small molecule.
  \item Implemented virtual screening utilizing Smina, \textbf{identified 147 potential positive fragments using Smina's scoring function}, and generated linkers between selected fragments and the stable core-fragment of THZ1.
